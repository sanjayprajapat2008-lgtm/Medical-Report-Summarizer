About MedReport

Empowering patients through clear, understandable health information

Our Mission

MedReport is dedicated to bridging the gap between complex medical terminology and patient understanding. We believe everyone has the right to comprehend their own health information clearly and confidently.

How It Works

1

Upload or Paste

Simply upload your medical report as a file or paste the text directly into our analyzer.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI system analyzes your report and identifies key findings, conditions, and metrics.

3

Plain English

We convert complex medical jargon into clear, easy-to-understand explanations.

4

Next Steps

Receive personalized recommendations on what to do next with your health information.

Privacy & Security

Your health information is extremely sensitive. We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • No data is stored on our servers
  • All processing is client-side
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • We never share your information with third parties
  • Your session data is cleared when you close your browser
